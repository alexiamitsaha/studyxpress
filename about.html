  • About Us

Study Xpress Consultants, incepted in 2009, is a Bangladeshi-based Overseas Educational Consultancy, dedicated to providing best solutions to the Bangladeshi students seeking for education in International educational institutions. Study Xpress is one of the Leading Overseas Educational Consultants, acknowledged for providing best guidance and counseling to the students. You can study abroad in best educational hubs of the world including UK, USA, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Malta, Czech Republic, Hungary, Poland, Sweden, China, South Korea, and Malaysia with us. As education is the main catalyst in deciding the career of a student, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring that you make the right decision. Our expert mentors understand the dilemma of Bangladeshi students and hence, have the par excellence in resolving all their doubts. Study Xpress keeps extensive knowledge of ever-changing education sector. With the valuable insights to deliver, we offer the best educational, cultural and financial solutions to the undergraduates, graduates and all the interested students.
Our team comprises the potential educators and counseling experts, along with a huge Alumni network to refer you to the top colleges and universities. We provide the complete assistance in Course counseling, University/Country selection, Scholarships, Education Loans, Pre-departure orientations. From form filling to fee submission, from the thought of studying abroad to your departure – we can help you at every step. Study Xpress has a right mix of professionals and young education enthusiasts; their exclusive, worthy and ideal knowledge about overseas studies, along with the right tactics to consult the aspirants makes us one of the most sought after Overseas Educational Consultancies in Bangladesh.

Why Choose Study Xpress?

If you are aspiring for a rewarding career in future and a world-class study program is quintessential for the fruition of your career plans, then trust Study Xpress - the pioneers in overseas education counseling - to equip you with the best possible academic opportunities in prestigious universities abroad.<
A leading education consultancy in Bangladesh, Study Xpress has successfully placed thousands of students in many illustrious universities all over the world – a feat that can be ascribed to our methodical mapping of geographical locations, institutions and courses as appropriate to the applicant’s profile.

1) Over 9 years of proven expertise in the overseas education counseling domain.
2) Bangladesh’s top-rated education consultancy with one of the highest student visa success rates in the industry.
3) Competent team of experienced and friendly counselors who guide students throughout the course admission process and help them successfully bridge the gap between where they are presently and where they aspire to be in future.
4) Student-centered strategy that emphasizes on personalized attention to each student as opposed to an assembly line approach.


In order to ensure the consistency in the quality of our services, we have consciously chosen not to operate through the conventional business model of franchisees/agents or branch offices. If you envision yourself as a successful professional in future and wondering how Study Xpress can help you transform your dreams into reality, then here’s a list of the standout offerings that provide Study Xpress with a competitive edge and make it your ideal partner in your academic pursuits that will play a vital role in shaping your career ahead:

1) Meticulous evaluation of students on various parameters such as academic background, financial stability, future plans, strengths and weaknesses to determine the most conducive options that are in line with their expectations.
2) Trusted partner for many students who rely solely upon our expert counseling and guidance to make informed decisions for a fulfilling professional career in the days to come.
